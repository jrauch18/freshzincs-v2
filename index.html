Fresh Zincs

The Fresh Zincs Platform is the first end-to-end blockchain-powered marketplace designed exclusively for pre-owned marine vessels, delivering unparalleled data integrity and transparency. ​Every listing on Fresh Zincs is backed by a rigorously standardized survey commissioned by the seller and conducted only by certified, network-vetted surveyors. Those digital surveys are cryptographically signed and immutably recorded on a high-throughput blockchain (e.g., Polygon), so buyers can instantly verify authenticity and provenance with zero reliance on third-party intermediaries.

Certified Vessel Surveys

Every listing on Fresh Zincs is backed by a rigorously standardized survey, performed only by network-vetted, certified marine surveyors.

Blockchain-Powered Verification

All surveys are cryptographically signed and immutably recorded on Polygon, so buyers can instantly confirm authenticity with zero reliance on third parties.

OCR-Driven Deep Search

Our OCR pipeline extracts every line-item—hull specs, engine hours, safety checks—and indexes it into a fully searchable database.

“Serious Buyers Only” Marketplace

By mandating pre-commissioned surveys, we eliminate 50% of unqualified inquiries, creating a streamlined, high-confidence buying experience.
